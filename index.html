Our Story

Nestled in the Heart of Lalitpur

Gokyo Bistro was born from a simple idea — that great food deserves a great setting. Since 2018, we have welcomed guests seeking a moment of calm, connection, and culinary delight in Jhamsikhel.

Our chefs draw inspiration from Nepal's rich culinary heritage, crafting dishes that honour tradition while embracing contemporary technique.
